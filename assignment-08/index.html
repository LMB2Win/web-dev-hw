Very Basic Skyrim Modding Guide



How to use Mod Organizer to download mods

Step 1 Download Mod Organizer
Step 2 Install or extract the program to somewhere on your computer
Step 3 Download your selected mods from the Skyrim Nexus
Step 4 Install mods in Mod Organizer by double clicking them in the Install pane
Step 5 Activate the mods in the left pane by clicking the circle
